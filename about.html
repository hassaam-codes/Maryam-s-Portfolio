About Me

I'm Maryam Rafique, a Graphic Designer based in Lahore who turns brand ideas into visuals people remember. My work spans branding, typography, and social media design, built on hands on experience with brands like Novex Studio, MSN Leathers, and Nation-Wide, leading creative projects end to end and collaborating with teams to ship visual content that performs.

I care about the details most people scroll past: the kerning, the grid, the color that makes a post stop the scroll. Every project starts with the same question, what does this brand need to say, and how do we say it visually. Let's work together to bring your brand to life.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. If you're interested in collaborating or have any inquiries, feel free to reach out, I'd love to bring your vision to life.

Looking forward to working together!
